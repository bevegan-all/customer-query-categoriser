### Sample Queries for AI Testing

Use these sample queries to test the accuracy of the Gemini 2.5 Flash categorization logic.
Each query below is designed to be mapped to a specific category.

### Category: Billing
1. "I was charged $49.99 twice on my credit card this month. Please refund the duplicate charge."
2. "Where can I find the invoice for my latest annual subscription renewal?"
3. "I want to cancel my Pro plan immediately, please don't charge me for next month."
4. "The pricing page says $10/mo but my receipt shows $15. Why is there a discrepancy?"
5. "My credit card expired and my payment failed. How do I update my payment method?"
